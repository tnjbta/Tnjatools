Quick answers

Will this workshop make me a licensed nail tech?

No — and anyone who tells you a 3-day class can is selling you a dream. This workshop builds real skills across every major nail system, and you earn a TNJA certificate of completion — but a certificate is not a license. Licensure in Virginia requires an approved program plus the state board exam. If you catch the bug, TNJA runs full licensure cohorts, and workshop attendees get a special offer when they enroll.

Who can attend?

Everyone from total beginners to self-taught nail lovers. We run a teen class (ages 13–19) and an adult class (ages 20+) as separate sessions. Teens need a parent or guardian to complete registration.

What's included in the price?

Three days of instruction (two in person, one live virtual refresher), all products and tools used in class, and your full take-home starter kit — including a professional nail drill.

How does the buddy discount work?

You and a friend register for the same session and list each other's names on the form — you each get $100 off. Don't have a buddy? Select the "match me with a buddy" option when you register: you secure your seat immediately and we'll work to pair you with a practice partner. If we match you before your workshop, the $100 discount comes off your remaining balance; if a match isn't found, the standard price applies. By choosing this option you agree to pay the standard remaining balance if no buddy match is made. We'll confirm your exact remaining balance before Day 1.

How does the virtual day work?

Day 3 is a live online session — review, troubleshooting, and Q&A. You'll get the link by email. All you need is your kit, your practice work, and a device with a camera.

Where are the in-person days held?

At The Nail Juice Academy — 2715 Monticello Ave., Suite D, Norfolk, VA 23517.

How do I pay?

Your $150 reservation fee is paid online — we accept credit and debit cards, plus Cash App Pay, processed securely through Square. Your remaining balance is due in cash, in the exact amount, on Day 1. Change may not be available.

How does the $150 reservation fee work?

You reserve your seat online with a $150 reservation fee. Reservation fees are nonrefundable and nontransferable. Your remaining balance is due in cash, in the exact amount, on Day 1 of your workshop — change may not be available. Adult class ($695): $545, or $445 with the buddy discount. Teen class ($595): $445, or $345 with the buddy discount.

What if I need to reschedule?

Reservation fees are nonrefundable and nontransferable, and they hold your seat for your registered session only. If you're unable to attend and want to join a future session, a new reservation fee is required to secure a new seat. If TNJA ever needs to move a session date, your reservation fee transfers to the new date automatically.
